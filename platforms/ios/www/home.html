FREECYCLE



MAKE OFFERS. PUBLISH NEEDS. DONATE. FEEL GOOD.



The Freecycle platform is primarily intended to help people announce emergencies and seek community help. It also provides a platform to recycle common household stuff to ineterested recipients. It intends to help people in need with urgent medical supplies, books for children, food for street children and other support through community involvement.

 

Features:


  • Browse Offers by City or Distance from you current location. 
  • View local emergencies.
  • View common household and other needs. 
  • Create Offers, emergencies and needs. 
  • Subscribe to events like urgent medical needs and other emergencies or for items of interest. e.g if you need a phone, subscribe to event category electronics to get notified of offers made.

Organizations like blood banks, hospitals and other services can use this platform to publish emergency needs. NGOs can use the platform to publish needs and collect articles and donations.
