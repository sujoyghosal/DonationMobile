FREECYCLE



MAKE OFFERS. PUBLISH NEEDS. DONATE. FEEL GOOD.



The Freecycle platform is primarily intended to recycle common household stuff for free to underprivileged recipients, though anyone in need can use the platform. It intends to help BPL people with urgent medical supplies, books for children, food for street children and other support through community involvement.

 

Features:


  • Browse Offers by City or Distance from you current location. 
  • View local emergencies.
  • View common household and other needs. 
  • Create Offers, emergencies and needs. 
  • Subscribe to events like urgent medical needs and other emergencies or for items of interest. e.g if you need a phone, subscribe to event category electronics to get notified of offers made.

NGOs are welcome to use the platform to collect donations and help distribute to the needy in your areas. Corporates can help sustain the efforts and achieve meaningful help to real needy through sponsorship of logistics like collecting stuff and dispersing to the needy.
